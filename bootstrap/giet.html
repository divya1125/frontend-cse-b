LIBRARY

Digital Library will be set up to preserve and provide instant access to knowledge generated within the campus for the overall development of GIET School of Pharmacy. Digital Library is functioning as institutional repository and covers full text resources like faculty and students publications, power point presentations, thesis, dissertation, academic projects, audio & video lectures, conference proceedings, events of photographs, news paper clippings and many more.

EVENTS

he Annual Techno Fest is the ideal combination of technology, joy, and culture. We organize elaborate project exhibitions for everyone to follow. It helps them to foster innovations with the potential for tremendous change. Nonetheless, the Annual Techno Fest does not confine itself to its domain. Techno Fest encompasses a diverse range of interests from students with innovative ideas. Every year, we encourage students from India to engage in creative and technical events. These include art shows, paintings and photography, music and dance, robotics, theatre and sports.

HOSTEL FECILITIES

GIET provides separate boarding facilities for boys and girls. The two hostels on the campus are well-managed and maintained in a very well organized manner with dedicated staff and close surveillance. Spacious living rooms with attached bathrooms and neatly laid out sparkling tiled floors make sure on-campus stay truly comfortable. Spotless dining halls and kitchens conforming to stringent hygienic norms complement to assure the inmates a healthy and reassuring dining experience. The hostels also provide space and facilities for entertainment, fitness and pursuit of personal hobbies..
